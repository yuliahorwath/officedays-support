Office Days - Support

About

Office Days helps you automatically track office visits and plan your hybrid work schedule with complete privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an office location?

Go to the Locations tab, enter your office address or use your current location, then tap "Add Location". The app will automatically detect when you arrive at this location.

Why isn't my location being tracked?

Make sure you have enabled "Always" location permission. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Office Days and select "Always".

Is my location data private?

Yes! All your location and schedule data is stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to any servers or shared with anyone. Your privacy is guaranteed.

How do I plan office days in advance?

On the Calendar tab, tap dates to select them, then tap a status tile (Office, PTO, Holiday, Credit, or WFH) to apply it to the selected days.

How do I delete a logged office visit?

Go to the Log tab and tap "Delete" next to any entry you want to remove.

Can I track multiple office locations?

Yes! Add as many office locations as you need in the Locations tab. The app will track visits to any of them.
